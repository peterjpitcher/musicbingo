Text fitting strategy (in the actual PDF)

  • Step 1 — Word wrap: Break text into lines at word boundaries to fit within cell width
  • Step 2 — Shrink font: If wrapped lines don't fit vertically, reduce font from 9pt down to 5pt (was 6pt)
  • Step 3 — Break words: If a single word is still too wide at minimum font, break it mid-word with a hyphen (e.g. "Super-stition")
  • Step 4 — Truncate: Only as absolute last resort, truncate with "…" — should almost never happen in landscape
  • Card 5 above shows how longer names like "Sabrina Carpenter" and "Earth, Wind & Fire" scale down slightly to fit
